Eryk's Hardware

Reviews!

i7 8700K

Eryk Gloginski

The i7 8700K is primarily used for gaming but can also be used for rendering videos.

It comes with 6 cores and 12 threads which can run constantly at 4.7 GHz.

The performance for it's price of 300 Euro is just amazing! It's even way cheaper than when it first came out!

I recommend it to you if would like to have a gaming centered machine.

Noctua NH-U12S

Anthony Tinsley

The Noctua NH-U12S is a really good cooler for the price you pay.

It can easily take care of any CPU even the i9 9900K! It's not even hard to install it!

I personally use it and all I can say is that I love how it keeps a CPU that is supposed to be water cooled cool.

It's about 90 Euro in total and it comes with 120mm 2 fans.
